The planet is in trouble, and we need to act fast to figure out a solution. Isodope, the online persona of Isabelle Boemeke, has been a dedicated advocate of nuclear energy as a safe and clean solution, and in 2025 she published a book gathering her 10+ years of research. We partnered with Isabelle to design the book's cover and title. We drew inspiration from the dynamic flow of energy at the core of nuclear power to create a colorful, eye-catching gradient reminiscent of a heatmap. It spreads across both covers and paints the spine in blue to make it pop amidst other books. The silver lettering for the title and other text grounds the colors and boldly claims Isodope's vision for a Rad Future.
