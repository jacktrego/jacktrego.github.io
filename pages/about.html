About

Jack Trego is a Creative Producer based in New York City, working at the intersection of brand, design, and production. He is currently at &Walsh, where he manages full rebrands, packaging, campaigns, typeface development, and TVC productions.

He also manages Type of Feeling, a type foundry creating fonts inspired by obscure and untranslatable feelings.
